Why Video Upload?

Because your videos deserve to look exactly as you made them.

YouTube and Vimeo force every video into a 16:9 player. If your content is square, vertical, or any non-standard ratio, you get ugly black bars on the sides. On top of that, external players add branding, titles, channel names, and suggested videos you can’t control.

This module adds an Upload Video tab directly inside Odoo’s media dialog — the same panel you already use for images, documents, and icons. Upload an MP4, WebM, or OGG file and it plays natively in an HTML5 <video> tag that respects the file’s original aspect ratio. No iframes, no third-party scripts, no branding overlays.

Key Features

▶

Native HTML5 Player

Videos play in a standard <video> tag. The player respects your video’s natural aspect ratio — no forced 16:9, no black bars.
⇧

Upload Tab in Media Dialog

A new Upload Video tab appears alongside Images, Documents, Icons, and Videos. No new UI to learn.
⚙

Autoplay, Loop & Controls

Toggle autoplay (auto-muted per browser policy), loop, and show/hide player controls directly in the upload panel.
🔒

No External Services

Videos are stored as Odoo attachments. No YouTube branding, no Vimeo suggested videos, no third-party tracking on your site.
📱

Mobile Friendly

The playsinline attribute ensures videos play inline on mobile devices instead of jumping to fullscreen.
🗃

MP4, WebM & OGG

Upload any browser-supported video format. The file picker is pre-filtered so users only see compatible files.

See It in Action

A closer look at how the module works.

Upload Video Tab

The new Upload Video tab sits right next to Images, Documents, Icons, and Videos in Odoo’s media dialog. Pick a file and it uploads instantly.

Video Options Panel

After uploading, toggle autoplay, loop, and player controls with simple switches. A live preview shows exactly what visitors will see.

Native Playback

The video displays in a clean HTML5 player that respects the original aspect ratio. No black bars, no external branding, no suggested videos.

Square & Vertical Videos

1:1 square videos, 9:16 vertical content, or any custom ratio — the player adapts to the file. No more fighting with 16:9 constraints.

Live on Your Website

The final result on your public website — a clean, self-hosted video that loads fast and looks exactly the way you intended.
